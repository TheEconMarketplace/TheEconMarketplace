Our Vision
The Econ Marketplace plans to establish a fully functional international marketplace where students offer the services with one another and gain hands-on experience being a business firm individually in the economic display. TEM wishes to establish a strong foundation for each student in economics and law that will stay with them even after their high school years.
Our Mission
The Econ Marketplace (TEM) provides a space for students who are interested in economics, law, and finance to learn the basic principles ranging from supply and demand to how an economy is affected by the laws of each country. TEM is a community for students to practice their skills and enhance their knowledge through a REAL-TIME marketplace, and a space for teachers and professionals to share their knowledge and expertise (through seminars, courses, and more!) with students and give well-established advice.
Our Values
—Tenacity
Participants, volunteers, and officers must display tenacity in interacting with the community actively— the tenacity to learn and ask, and the tenacity to share vital information. Remember, persistence and confidence is key!
—Education
TEM is built on education! Here at TEM, we build a healthy and engaged space through educating each other on the various fields students at TEM are interested in.
—Mindful
All of moderation comes with mindfulness. It starts with you— the moment you ask for advice, discuss your opinions, and reply to your peers, mindfulness should be the first priority in using TEM.
